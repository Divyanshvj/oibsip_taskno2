Rohit Sharma

Indian Cricketer

About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. He is a right-handed batsman and an occasional right-arm off-break bowler. Rohit made his debut for the Indian cricket team in June 2007. He is known for his elegant strokeplay and is considered one of the best opening batsmen in the world.

Career

Rohit Sharma has played for the Indian cricket team in all three formats of the game: Test, One Day International (ODI), and Twenty20 International (T20I). He has scored over 10,000 runs in ODI cricket and is the only player to have scored three double centuries in the format. In T20I cricket, he is the second-highest run-scorer of all time.

Achievements

  • Has won the Indian Premier League (IPL) title 5 times with the Mumbai Indians
  • Scored 3 double centuries in ODI cricket, the highest by any player
  • Scored 264 runs in an ODI match against Sri Lanka, the highest individual score in the format
  • Won the Arjuna Award in 2015 and the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna in 2020
